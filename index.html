About

Cassete Tape is a html 5 hiphop drum loop player and voice recorder. Perfect to record a freestyle in a window browser. You just need to press play to listen to the hiphop loop and when you feel ready just press rec and it starts recording your voice. After that a wav file with your freestyle is generated and you can save on your computer.

This is a Chrome Experiment. It was developed to work only in this browser. It uses some technologies not yet fully supported by all browsers.

Technology

  • Web Audio API
